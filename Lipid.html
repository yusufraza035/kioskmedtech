Lipid Analysis Meter

Ensure you have test strips and lancets ready for use.

Clean the fingertip (or venous draw site) with an alcohol swab.

Use a sterile lancet to prick the fingertip and obtain a small drop of blood.

Apply the blood sample by gently touching the test strip to the drop of blood.

The meter will display results on the screen within a few seconds to a few minutes.
