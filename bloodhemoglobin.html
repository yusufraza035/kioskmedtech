Blood Haemoglobin meter

Clean the fingertip with an alcohol swab and allow it to dry.

Use a sterile lancet to prick the fingertip and collect a small blood droplet.

Touch the test strip to the blood sample until it fills the strip.

Wait for the results the device will process the sample and display hemoglobin levels within a few seconds.
